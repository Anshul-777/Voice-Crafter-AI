Everything Voice AI Needs

Four powerful pillars covering the complete voice AI lifecycle — from cloning to generation, detection to deployment.

XTTS-v2

Voice Cloning

Clone any voice from just 3 seconds of audio with zero-shot XTTS-v2 technology. Fine-tune for professional-grade fidelity and manage speaker profiles with full version control.

  • Zero-shot cloning from 3+ seconds of audio
  • Fine-tune mode for maximum voice similarity
  • Speaker embedding extraction & versioning
  • Audio quality pre-analysis via Quality Lab
  • Public / private voice profiles with metadata
17 Languages

Voice Generation (TTS)

Generate expressive, natural-sounding speech in 17 languages. Control emotion, speed, pitch, and temperature with granular precision. Stream audio live over WebSocket.

  • 8 emotions: neutral, happy, sad, excited, calm, professional, storytelling, news
  • Speed, pitch & temperature controls
  • SSML support on Pro & Enterprise plans
  • Streaming audio output via WebSocket
  • WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG output formats
5-Model Ensemble

Deepfake Detection

Industry-leading 5-model ensemble catches voice deepfakes with unmatched accuracy. Real-time streaming analysis, batch processing, and forensic-grade chain-of-custody evidence logs.

  • Real-time mic stream detection via WebSocket
  • Batch file analysis with confidence timeline
  • Per-speaker diarization & fraud scoring
  • Chain-of-custody evidence logs & JSON export

How It Works

From Audio to Intelligence

Three steps to unlock the full power of voice AI — whether cloning, generating, or detecting at scale.

01

Upload or Record

Provide 3+ seconds of audio via file upload, live microphone stream, or direct API call. Quality Lab instantly analyzes SNR, speech ratio, and clipping.

02

AI Processing

XTTS-v2 extracts voice embeddings for cloning. The 5-model ensemble simultaneously analyzes spectral, prosodic, and glottal features for deepfake detection.

03

Generate or Detect

Receive your cloned voice profile, synthesized audio in any format, or a full forensic report with per-model confidence scores and chain-of-custody evidence log.
